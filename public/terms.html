Terms of service.

Plain-English terms for booking and receiving pool services from us.

These terms apply when you book a service through tqpoolservices.au or otherwise engage TQ Pool Services. By booking with us, you agree to them. Anything unclear — email info@tqpoolservices.com.

Who we are

TQ Pool Services is a mobile pool servicing business operating from 2 Allunga St, Kelso QLD 4815, Australia. Contact: 0488 355 111 / info@tqpoolservices.com.

Services we offer

  • Consultation — a 30-minute site visit to assess your pool, equipment and chemistry. Priced at AUD $35, paid up front via Stripe at time of booking.
  • Basic Pool Service — water testing, chemical dosing, surface and filter clean, equipment inspection. Priced by pool size; the final price is confirmed on arrival and invoiced through QuickBooks the same day.
  • Equipment maintenance, repair and product supply — quoted individually.

Booking and confirmation

When you book online, the slot is reserved as soon as we receive your details. For the paid Consultation, your slot is held while you complete Stripe Checkout; if you don't finish payment within 30 minutes, the slot is released for someone else.

For a Basic Pool Service, the slot is locked the moment you submit the booking — no payment is required up front.

We'll send a confirmation email shortly after booking and a reminder before the visit.

Pricing and payment

All prices are in Australian dollars (AUD) and include GST where applicable. Payments are processed by Stripe; we never see or store your card details.

  • Consultation: paid online via Stripe Checkout at booking.
  • Basic Pool Service: invoiced via QuickBooks Online after the visit. Pay via the "Pay Now" link in the invoice email or by bank transfer.
  • Equipment, products and repairs: invoiced separately, terms shown on each quote.

Cancellations and reschedules

  • Free reschedule up to 12 hours before your slot. Email or call us and we'll find another window.
  • Within 12 hours — we may charge a small call-out fee, especially if we've already loaded the van.
  • Refunds for paid Consultations are processed via Stripe and usually clear in 5–10 business days.

What we need from you

  • Access to the pool — gate unlocked, dogs secured, parking available within a reasonable distance.
  • Accurate address and a working phone number in case we need to reach you on the day.
  • If access requires a code or special instructions, please add them to the booking form.

If we arrive and can't access the pool through no fault of our own, a call-out fee may apply.

Our service standards

  • We arrive within the 2-hour window you booked, weather and traffic permitting.
  • We use trusted brands (Astralpool, Zodiac, Fluidra, MagnaPool, Viron) and current best-practice chemistry.
  • You receive a photo and chemical report by email after every visit.
  • If something we do during the service damages your pool, we'll fix it or refund it. Reasonable wear and pre-existing issues aren't our responsibility — we'll flag them and quote separately.

Limitations and disclaimers

To the extent permitted by Australian law:

  • We're not liable for indirect, consequential or incidental loss arising from the service.
  • Our liability for any direct loss is capped at the total amount paid for that specific service.
  • We rely on the information you provide about your pool. If the actual pool differs materially from what you told us (size, equipment, condition), the visit may take longer or require an adjusted quote.

None of this excludes any non-excludable guarantees you have under the Australian Consumer Law.

Service area

We're mobile across Townsville and surrounding suburbs (Kelso, Kirwan, Annandale, Hyde Park, Idalia, North Ward, Mundingburra, Aitkenvale, Hermit Park, Mt Louisa, Bushland Beach and more). If you're outside that area, get in touch — we'll let you know what's possible.

Photos and reports

Photos you upload through the booking form are used to size the job and bring the right equipment. They're stored alongside your booking and deleted once the job is complete, unless we've separately agreed to keep one as a before/after example (we'll ask first).

The chemical/photo report we send after each service is for your records — you're free to share it with whoever needs it.

Changes to these terms

If we update these terms, the "last updated" date below changes and the new version applies to bookings made after that date. Existing bookings keep the terms that were in effect when you booked.

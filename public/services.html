FAQs

Quick questions.

Do I need to be home during the service?

No — as long as we have access to the pool area. Most clients leave a side gate unlocked or provide a code. We send before & after photos so you know exactly what was done.

What suburbs do you cover?

Townsville and surrounds — Kirwan, Annandale, Hyde Park, Idalia, North Ward, Mundingburra, Aitkenvale, Hermit Park, Mt Louisa, Bushland Beach. Outside that? Get in touch — we'll often travel further for corporate jobs.

How do payments work?

You can pay by card at booking via our secure Stripe checkout, or set up a saved card for ongoing services. Invoices are issued via QuickBooks and emailed automatically.

Can I skip or reschedule a service?

Yes — just let us know at least 24 hours ahead. You'll have an account where you can view, reschedule, or pause upcoming services.

What if my pool needs a chemical top-up?

Standard chemicals (chlorine, pH adjusters, stabiliser) are included as part of regular servicing where used in normal quantities. Larger doses (e.g. a shock treatment for an algae bloom) are quoted separately and approved by you before we proceed.

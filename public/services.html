Local servicing

Pool service in Townsville is its own thing.

North Queensland puts pools through more than the southern states ever see. Long stretches of tropical heat drive evaporation and chlorine demand up. The wet season dumps leaves, frangipani, mango blossom and storm runoff into open water week after week. Salt-air on the coastal side eats away at chlorinator cells, pump seals and filter housings faster than the manufacturer manuals suggest. None of that is a problem with the right cadence — it only becomes a problem when a pool is left to drift between visits.

A regular Townsville pool service rotation typically runs fortnightly through summer and can stretch to four-weekly in the cooler months. A weekly schedule suits holiday rentals, family pools with heavy use, and anything sitting under a big mango or palm canopy. Whatever the cadence, the visit looks the same: a full clean, a complete chemical balance, equipment and basket check, backwash or cartridge clean as required, and a written report that's actually readable.

Because we're a mobile pool service, everything turns up in the van — vacuum, brushes, test kit, dosing chemicals, cartridge cleaner. There is nothing for you to stock beyond a few basics, and there is nothing to drive to. Most of our regulars leave the side gate unlocked while they're at work; we send before-and-after photos so you know exactly what was done. If chemistry is drifting we flag it on the report and act before it turns into a green pool.

FAQs

Quick questions.

Do I need to be home during the service?

No — as long as we have access to the pool area. Most clients leave a side gate unlocked or share a code. We send before-and-after photos so you know exactly what was done while you were out.

What Townsville suburbs do you cover?

The CBD plus Kirwan, Annandale, Hyde Park, Idalia, North Ward, Mundingburra, Aitkenvale, Hermit Park, Mt Louisa and Bushland Beach. Outside that? Drop us a line — we often travel further for corporate jobs.

What if my pool needs a chemical top-up?

Standard chemicals (chlorine, pH adjusters, stabiliser) are included as part of regular Townsville pool servicing in normal quantities. Larger doses — say a shock treatment for an algae bloom — are quoted separately and approved by you before we proceed.

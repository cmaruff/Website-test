What's included

The full visit, every time.

Every Basic Pool Service in Townsville follows the same checklist — no variations based on cadence. Whether it's your weekly visit or a one-off clean, the work that gets done is the same. The price changes with pool size, not corners cut.

  • Water testing — chlorine, pH, alkalinity, salt, cyanuric acid
  • Chemical dosing to bring readings into range
  • Surface clean — vacuum, brush, skimmer + pump baskets emptied
  • Filter clean — backwash or cartridge wash as required
  • Equipment check — pump, chlorinator cell, timer, return jets
  • Photo + written chemistry report emailed when done

Service FAQs

Quick questions, before you book.

How often should I book a Basic Pool Service in Townsville?

Most North Queensland pools sit happiest on a fortnightly visit through summer (Oct–Apr) and four-weekly through cooler months. Weekly makes sense for holiday rentals, family pools with heavy use, or anything under a big mango or palm canopy that drops debris constantly.

Do I need to be home for the visit?

No. Most regulars leave a side gate unlocked or share a code. We send before-and-after photos with the report so you know exactly what was done.

What chemicals do you use — and are they included?

Standard chlorine, pH adjusters (acid, soda ash), stabiliser, salt. Chemicals are billed separately at supplier cost — we show you what's needed and authorise it before adding anything. No hardware-store mark-up.

What if my pool's bigger than 100,000 L?

That's a tailored quote rather than a tier — get in touch with the rough dimensions and we'll work out timing and pricing.

Can I reschedule once I've booked?

Yes — free reschedules up to 12 hours before the visit. Inside that window a small reschedule fee applies because we've already routed the day.

Do you handle body corporate / corporate pools?

Yes — same standards, same chemistry approach. For multiple bodies of water on one site or a different reporting cadence, get in touch for a tailored quote.
